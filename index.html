About me

Hi! Thanks for stopping by! I was born and raised in the Bay Area, and I moved to Orange County in 2015 to attend UC Irvine. I started off there as a biology major, and quickly realized that I did not foresee a career in that field. My interest in development began when I took an introductory python course at a community college. I loved that programming allowed me to use my logical and creative skills hand in hand to create applications people could actually use! This lead me to ultimately pivot to full time web development. I look forward to continue to learn and grow with every opportunity that comes my way. Reach out, and lets’ build something together!

Featured Projects

Melleau

Live Site | Github

Melleau (pronounced: ‘mellow’) is a self-diagnostic mood tracking application designed as a platform to share your thoughts and feelings, with the goal of assisting you in bettering yourself emotionally, mentally, and spiritually. This app was created using React.js for the frontend and PHP and MySQL on the backend. My role in this project was the lead backend engineer. As the lead backend engineer I created all the endpoints for the app as well as set up the database. I also had the opportunity to work on the frontend, where I made a fully functional calendar.
